3CX and Welltech combine to offer fully supported low cost solution for analog lines

Partnership pairs 3CX’s best of breed Windows-based IP PBX with Welltech Analog VoIP Gateways for a fully interoperable end to end Business Telephony Solution.

LONDON UK and TAIPEI, TAIWAN, 04 DECEMBER 2013 – 3CX, developer of the award-winning Windows VoIP PBX 3CX Phone System, and Welltech, a leading VoIP gateway manufacturer in Taiwan, announce that through their strategic partnership, 3CX Phone System and Welltech gateways are fully interoperable and automatically configurable, guaranteeing 3CX customers and partners unparalleled support.

 

“Welltech gateways are reliable and easy to use and allow our resellers to lower the cost of the 3CX solution for customers that require connectivity to analog lines. 3CX resellers can now compete with low cost appliances on price, whilst still offering customers a higher quality unified communications solution. Our customers and partners will be provided with full support from both companies, giving them peace of mind,” said Nick Galea, 3CX CEO.

 

“3CX has a proven track record of being the leading Windows-based VoIP PBX and it’s our aim to provide our customers and partners with an affordable and reliable gateway that works seamlessly with 3CX Phone System,” said Hank Lin, Welltech CEO. “Our partnership with 3CX guarantees that Welltech gateways are easily configurable with 3CX Phone System, ensuring 3CX Partners an effortless installation.”

Welltech Gateways and 3CX Phone System - Guaranteed Interop

Advanced integration and full interoperability between 3CX Phone System and Welltech gateways are guaranteed with this partnership. Before any new firmware is made available for the Welltech gateways, both 3CX and Welltech will test the firmware on the latest version of 3CX Phone System to ensure that Welltech gateways are fully interoperable and automatically configured with 3CX Phone System. 3CX and Welltech also provide guaranteed response times for any interop issues that may occur.

Keep existing analog lines with Welltech VoIP Gateways

In many cases companies still require their analog phone lines to be connected to the PBX and the only way to achieve this is by using a VoIP gateway. Welltech gateways are affordable and reliable and can provide 3CX Phone System users with the ability  to virtualize 3CX Phone System with Hyper-V or VMware.
